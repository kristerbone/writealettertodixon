# Draft 1: Official Diplomatic Transmission from the Arcturian High Council

**Style:** A formal, dead-serious diplomatic letter — as if the Arcturian Star Council has convened specifically to address the matter of getting backstage at DC10.

---

**CLASSIFIED — PRIORITY: MAXIMUM GROOVE**

**FROM:** The Office of Interdimensional Cultural Affairs, Arcturian High Council, Star System Arcturus, Boötes Constellation

**TO:** His Excellency, DIXON — Guardian of the Low End, Keeper of the Groove, Sovereign of the Dancefloor

**RE:** Formal Request for Backstage VIP & DJ Booth Access — DC10 Circoloco Opening Weekend, Ibiza, Earth

**DATE:** Stardate 2026.04 (Earth Calendar)

---

Dear Most Honourable Dixon,

We write to you on behalf of the Arcturian High Council — a governing body of fifth-dimensional light beings from the star Arcturus, located 36.6666666666666 light-years from your planet in the Boötes constellation.

After centuries of monitoring Earth's radio frequencies, television broadcasts, and — more recently — your SoundCloud page, our Council has reached a unanimous conclusion:

**You are the one.**

Your mastery of deep, hypnotic grooves has transcended the boundaries of mere Earthly entertainment. The frequencies you produce have been detected by our long-range harmonic sensors, and frankly, they have caused several disruptions to our meditation schedules. Elders who have maintained perfect stillness for 400 light years have been caught doing subtle head-nods.

This is unprecedented.

We have therefore dispatched two of our finest Ambassadors to the island you call "Ibiza" to attend the Opening Weekend at DC10. Their mission is threefold:

1. **To experience your set in its full terrestrial glory** — our simulations, while impressive, cannot replicate the energy of 2,000 humans losing their minds to your live transmissions.
2. **To gain access to the backstage VIP area and DJ booth** 
3. **To extend an invitation** for you to perform at the Arcturian Intergalactic Music Summit (capacity: 4 billion conscious beings across 12 dimensions). The fee is negotiable, but we should mention that our currency is pure vibrational energy, which on Earth roughly converts to "good vibes."

We understand this mission is unusual. We assure you it is entirely sincere. Our Ambassadors will be easy to identify — they will be the ones dancing with an inexplicable cosmic glow, speaking in reverent tones about your all night long sets, and possibly levitating slightly during the peak hours.

Should you grant this request, the Arcturian High Council will formally recognise Earth as a "Level 3 Groove Civilisation" — a significant upgrade from your current classification of "Level 1: Promising but Still Plays Mr. Brightside at Weddings."

We eagerly await your response.

With infinite respect, deep bass, and unconditional love from across the cosmos,

**The Arcturian High Council**
